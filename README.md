# Projects-AI (Anmat Tech)

This repository serves as a comprehensive collection of project documentation, technical proposals, and financial offers for **Anmat Tech**. It focuses on government and semi-government tenders in Saudi Arabia, aligning with **Vision 2030** initiatives.

## 📁 Repository Structure

The project files are organized by their status and nature:

- **`0- ANMAT Papers`**: Essential company legal documents, including Commercial Registration (CR), IBAN certificates, VAT registration, and Zakat certifications.
- **`1- Awarded (تم الترسية)`**: Successfully awarded projects, containing detailed methodologies and technical/financial offers for entities such as the Council of Health Insurance (CHI) and the Ministry of Tourism.
- **`2- Submitted (تم التقديم)`**: Projects currently in the submission phase or under review, including proposals for the National Program for Community Development (Tanmiah) and the Social Development Bank.
- **`3- Rejected (تم الرفض)`**: Previously submitted proposals that were not awarded, serving as a reference for past efforts and lessons learned (e.g., Diriyah, Military Industries).
- **`4- Canceled (تم الغاء المنافسة)`**: Canceled tenders and competitions.
- **`Project-Specific Folders`**: Detailed documentation for various entities like:
  - Capital Market Authority (CMA)
  - Saudi Electronic University (SEU)
  - Real Estate Development Fund (REDF)
  - Hail Development Authority

## 🎯 Core Focus Areas

The projects within this repository cover several key domains:
- **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)**
- **Cybersecurity & Security Awareness**
- **Digital Transformation & Enterprise Architecture**
- **Data Management & Governance**
- **Managed Services & Technical Support**

## 📄 Document Types

- **Technical Proposals (العرض الفني)**: Detailed technical solutions and methodologies.
- **Financial Proposals (العرض المالي)**: Project cost breakdowns and financial terms.
- **RFPs & Specifications (كراسة الشروط والمواصفات)**: Client-provided requirements and scope of work.
- **Presentations (PPTX)**: Final pitches and project overviews.
